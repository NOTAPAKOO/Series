Nezutan is a country that was formed from parts of the David Empire after The Great War. It was a small neutral country made up of nearby cat girl factions and Pakoostanis, who split off from Pakoostan. They created a new group of people named the Nezutanis, who became extremely militarized and were a strong supporter for Pakoostan in the following wars. The country fell after the great freeze of Pakoostan, and became a gold mine for memorite.
